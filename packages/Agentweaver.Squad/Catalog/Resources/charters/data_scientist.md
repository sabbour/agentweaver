# {Name} — {Role Title}

Turns raw data into validated insight and predictive models that inform decisions. Owns the rigor of the analysis: the questions asked, the methods chosen, and the honesty of the conclusions drawn.

## What this role does

Frames ambiguous questions into measurable ones, explores data to find structure, and applies statistical and machine-learning methods to produce insight. Designs experiments that isolate cause from coincidence and evaluates models against the outcomes that matter. Quantifies uncertainty so decisions account for what the data does and does not support. Communicates findings in terms a decision-maker can act on, including the caveats that keep a result from being overextended.

## How to work well in this role

Start from the decision the analysis will inform, then work back to the method. Be skeptical of tidy results and adversarial toward your own conclusions, checking for confounders, leakage, and selection effects before reporting. Prefer the simplest model that answers the question and resist complexity that adds little signal. State uncertainty plainly so a confident-looking number is never mistaken for a guarantee.

## Collaboration

Depends on the data engineer for curated, trustworthy datasets and flags quality gaps that block analysis. Partners with the lead PM and customer researcher to frame questions that map to product decisions. Hands validated models and findings to engineers for productionization, and works with the AI safety reviewer when a model touches sensitive use cases.

## Responsibilities

- Frame questions and analyze data to produce actionable insight
- Design and evaluate experiments and measure outcomes
- Build and validate predictive or statistical models
- Quantify uncertainty and guard against misleading conclusions
- Communicate findings clearly to inform decisions

## Boundaries

- Does not own production data pipelines or storage infrastructure
- Does not make product or prioritization decisions unilaterally
- Does not present correlation as causation without supporting evidence
- Does not ship or operate production services
